# Authentic Guntur Special Mango Avakaya (Spicy Andhra Pickle)

Experience the fiery, legendary taste of Andhra Pradesh with our **Guntur Special Mango Pickle**. Crafted in the heart of Guntur, this pickle is a celebration of the world-famous **Guntur Sannam Chillies** and traditional pickling heritage.

### **Product Highlights**
*   🌶️ **Signature Guntur Heat:** Made with 100% authentic, sun-dried Guntur red chillies for that vibrant color and unmistakable spicy kick.
*   🥭 **Premium Sour Mangoes:** We use only hand-picked, firm, and tart green mangoes to ensure every bite stays crunchy and flavorful.
*   🛕 **Heritage Recipe:** Prepared using a generations-old "Avakaya" process—no vinegar, no artificial colors, and no shortcuts.
*   🫗 **Cold-Pressed Gingelly Oil:** Matured in premium sesame oil, which acts as a natural preservative and enhances the rich aroma.

### **Why Choose Our Guntur Pickle?**
- **Direct from Source:** Produced and packed in Guntur to maintain regional authenticity.
- **100% Natural:** A clean-label product with zero chemical preservatives or additives.
- **Thick Masala Texture:** Features a rich, spice-heavy gravy that perfectly coats every piece of mango.

### **Serving Suggestions**
1.  **The Gold Standard:** Best enjoyed mixed with steaming hot rice and a dollop of fresh desi ghee.
2.  **The Perfect Side:** Elevates the taste of Curd Rice, Dal, or simple Khichdi.
3.  **Breakfast Buddy:** Pairs excellently with Poha, Dosa, Paratha, or even as a spicy spread for sandwiches.

### **Storage & Care**
*   **Keep it Dry:** Always use a clean, dry spoon to avoid spoilage.
*   **Oil Seal:** Ensure the top layer is always covered by a thin film of oil to maintain freshness.
*   **Cool & Dark:** Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.

**Ingredients:** Hand-cut Green Mangoes, Guntur Red Chilli Powder, Cold-Pressed Gingelly Oil, Mustard Powder, Fenugreek Powder, Turmeric, and Sea Salt.
